# Red Flag Checklist

Use this before scoring or recommending.

- [ ] Total delivered price is missing.
- [ ] Weight or selected option is unclear.
- [ ] Origin/manufacturer/importer is missing.
- [ ] Seller identity or business details are unclear.
- [ ] Listing depends on an ad, affiliate post, or unsupported ranking.
- [ ] Price is suspiciously cheap without evidence.
- [ ] Reviews are pooled across unrelated options.
- [ ] At least two recent reviews mention the same serious issue.
- [ ] Meat-specific risk appears: 잡내, 누린내, 비계 과다, 해동 배송, 포장 파손, 질김.
- [ ] Product appears different from the requested category.

If any critical item is checked, mark hold or avoid unless stronger evidence resolves it.
