# Ad And Sponsored Review Filtering

## What To Check

- Sponsored labels, affiliate disclosure, partner links, and coupon/referral codes
- Whether the page includes direct product-page evidence
- Whether claims are backed by marketplace or official source links
- Whether review language is generic or promotional

## Common Traps

- Blog posts that summarize without checking current price or seller
- Affiliate roundups ranking products without evidence
- Search ads presented as organic recommendations

## Strong Evidence

- Independent review discloses relationship and still links to verifiable product details.
- Claims are checkable against product pages and recent buyer reviews.

## Mark Unknown

- Unsupported quality claims
- Claims without source links
- Current price or seller details from non-product pages

## Output Implications

- Do not use ads or affiliate-only posts as primary evidence.
- Exclude recommendations that depend mainly on unsupported promotional claims.
