# Review Trust Signals

## What To Check

- Recent reviews over lifetime average rating
- Reviews tied to the exact product option
- Repeated negative categories
- Photo/video reviews showing package, meat condition, or delivery state
- Seller responses to problems

## Common Traps

- Reviews pooled across different weights or products
- Old high ratings hiding recent quality decline
- Incentivized reviews that say little about product condition

## Strong Evidence

- Multiple recent verified reviews mention the same strength or issue.
- Review text matches the selected product and delivery method.
- Photos support claims about packaging, thawing, or meat quality.

## Mark Unknown

- Sentiment based only on star rating
- Claims from a single isolated review
- Reviews that do not match the selected option

## Output Implications

- Treat a repeated negative issue as meaningful only after at least two independent recent reviews mention it.
- Separate review evidence from product page evidence.
