# Coupon And Subscription Prices

## What To Check

- Whether the displayed price requires coupon clipping, membership, subscription, app checkout, card benefit, or first-purchase status
- Whether the discount is repeatable
- Whether the user can realistically access the condition

## Common Traps

- Best price requires a one-time coupon.
- Subscription price is shown more prominently than one-time purchase price.
- Card discounts are platform-wide but not guaranteed for all users.

## Strong Evidence

- Product page clearly states the condition for the discounted price.
- Ordinary price and conditional price are both visible.

## Mark Unknown

- Coupon eligibility
- Subscription cancellation burden
- Card/member-only benefit if not visible in source

## Output Implications

- Compare ordinary delivered price first.
- Add conditional price only as a separate note.
- Do not rank a product first solely because of an uncertain coupon.
