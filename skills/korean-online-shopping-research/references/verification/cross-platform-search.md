# Cross-Platform Search

Do not end an unrestricted product search on one marketplace.

If the best candidate is HOLD, broaden the search before presenting it as the final answer.

Before final judgment, collect candidates from different source types when possible:

- Large marketplace
- Grocery/retail mall
- Private-label/PB product
- Brand/direct mall
- Specialist seller
- Naver Smart Store/open market
- External review/community result

Use multiple search vocabularies:

- Exact product words
- Use-case words
- Adjacent category words
- Brand/PB words
- Negative-review words

Aim for at least two different source types when possible.

A candidate becomes stronger when it survives comparison against alternatives, not when it wins one site's ranking.

Broad search should produce category-diverse candidates, not one mixed ranking. Compare within category first, then compare across categories.

The overall winner should explain whether it wins because of price, trust, use-case fit, or low regret risk.

If the overall winner is from an adjacent category, preserve and report the best candidate from the original/intended category.

If the search cannot be broadened or a source type is not searched, mark that as a limitation and keep the judgment conservative.
