# Cross-Platform Search

Do not end an unrestricted product search on one marketplace.

If the best candidate is HOLD, broaden the search before presenting it as the final answer. Check at least two different source types when possible:

- Large marketplace
- Brand/direct mall
- Specialist seller
- Grocery/retail mall
- Naver Smart Store

A candidate becomes stronger when it survives comparison against alternatives, not when it wins one site's ranking.

If the search cannot be broadened, mark that as missing evidence and keep the judgment conservative.
