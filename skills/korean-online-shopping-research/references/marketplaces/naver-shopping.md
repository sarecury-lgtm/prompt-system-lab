# Naver Shopping

## What To Check

- Whether the result is a marketplace listing, smart store, brand store, or ad
- Seller identity, store rating, and business information
- Product page price, delivery fee, bundle size, and weight
- Origin/manufacturer/importer fields
- Review recency and whether reviews match the exact product option

## Common Traps

- Search rankings can mix ads, affiliate content, and unrelated options.
- Option selectors can change weight and price.
- Reviews may combine multiple options or package sizes.

## Strong Evidence

- Smart store or brand page has clear seller identity and product details.
- Reviews are tied to the selected option.
- Price and shipping are visible without relying on teaser pricing.

## Mark Unknown

- Option-specific weight or price not visible
- Origin/source fields missing
- Reviews pooled across unrelated variants

## Output Implications

- Separate ad/search-result claims from product page evidence.
- Use the selected option only when calculating 100g price.
