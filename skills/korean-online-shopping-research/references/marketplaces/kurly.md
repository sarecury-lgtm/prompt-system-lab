# Kurly

## What To Check

- Product weight, storage type, origin, manufacturer, and seller/importer
- Delivery condition and cold-chain claims
- Review mentions of freshness, thawing, packaging, and smell
- Whether discounts require membership, coupons, or time-limited events

## Common Traps

- Premium presentation can make weak source details look stronger than they are.
- Discounted price may not be generally available.
- Reviews may emphasize recipe use rather than meat quality.

## Strong Evidence

- Clear origin/source and storage condition
- Recent reviews with consistent delivery and quality experience
- Transparent price and delivery terms

## Mark Unknown

- Membership-only effective price
- Missing manufacturer/importer
- Review evidence that does not discuss product condition

## Output Implications

- Treat source clarity and cold-chain evidence as important for meat.
- Mark coupon-only price separately from ordinary price.
