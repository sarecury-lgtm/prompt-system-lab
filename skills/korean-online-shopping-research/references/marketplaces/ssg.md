# SSG

## What To Check

- Whether the seller is SSG, a department/market partner, or a third-party seller
- Product weight, package count, origin, manufacturer, importer, and storage condition
- Delivery fee, delivery method, and cold-chain handling
- Recent reviews for delivery condition and quality

## Common Traps

- Large-platform trust does not automatically apply to every third-party listing.
- Promotions and card benefits may distort the real comparable price.
- Store pickup, early delivery, and parcel delivery can have different risk profiles.

## Strong Evidence

- Clear seller role and business details
- Visible delivery fee and weight
- Recent reviews match the exact meat product and option

## Mark Unknown

- Third-party seller reliability when store details are weak
- Price after card-only or member-only benefits
- Origin/manufacturer/importer if fields are blank

## Output Implications

- Compare ordinary delivered price first.
- List optional card/member discounts separately.
