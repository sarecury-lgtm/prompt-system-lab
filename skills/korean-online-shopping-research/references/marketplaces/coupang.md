# Coupang

## What To Check

- Rocket delivery / seller delivery distinction
- Seller name and business details
- Item price, required delivery fee, bundle size, and weight
- Product origin, manufacturer, importer, and storage condition
- Recent buyer reviews, not only star rating

## Common Traps

- Rocket badge can hide differences between product quality and seller reliability.
- Unit price can change after delivery fee, bundle count, or coupon conditions.
- Review count may be high but recent meat-quality complaints can still matter.

## Strong Evidence

- Product page clearly shows weight, origin, seller, storage type, and delivery fee.
- Recent verified reviews are consistent and mention meat condition after delivery.
- Seller information and return/refund rules are visible.

## Mark Unknown

- Unclear origin, manufacturer, importer, total grams, or delivery fee
- Review claims based only on star rating
- Seller reliability when business info is not visible

## Output Implications

- Exclude if total price or weight is unclear.
- Flag if recent reviews mention thawing, smell, damaged packaging, or excessive fat.
